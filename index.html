Clean by Nature, Verified by Testing

Every batch of rainwater is tested to ensure a stable and reliable composition. With low total dissolved solids (TDS) and a near-neutral pH, it delivers a clean and consistent drinking profile. Laboratory analysis shows naturally low mineral content — calcium, magnesium, and sodium — contributing to its smooth, light taste.

With sodium levels as low as approximately 0.75 mg/L, Tropicanna offers a noticeably lighter alternative to most bottled waters.

Water is part of every moment — from daily routines to shared experiences. Raindrop is designed to offer a clean, balanced drinking experience that fits effortlessly into modern spaces, without distraction or heaviness.
