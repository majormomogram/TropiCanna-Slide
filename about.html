01 / THE SHIFT

We looked up instead

While studying traditional water sources, extensive NAHRIM research led us to look elsewhere — upward.

We realised that true purity wasn't found by digging deeper, but by looking up.

We shifted our focus to the pristine atmosphere of Taman Negara, exploring what could be captured before it even touches the ground.

02 / THE APPROACH

From observation,
to experiment

We wanted to capture water in its purest state. This meant intercepting it before it made contact with the ground, deeply analyzing its composition, and understanding the natural purification that occurs quietly within the clouds.

03 / THE ENGINEERING

Engineered around nature

After years of rigorous development, we designed a collection system that honors the natural water cycle while ensuring absolute safety.

01

Uncompromised Purity

Harvested securely above the earth, eliminating the risk of ground contamination and pollutants.

02

Closed-Loop Collection

Captured directly from the sky in sterile conditions immediately sealed to protect its natural state.

03

Advanced Filtration

Gently refined to achieve an optimal mineral balance — naturally clean without altering its natural character.
